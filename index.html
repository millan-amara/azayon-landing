About Azayon

Built here.
Built for here.

Azayon is a CRM built specifically for African SMBs — not an afterthought port of a Western product, but something designed from the ground up for how businesses on this continent actually operate.

Why we built this

Every CRM tool we tried was built for companies in San Francisco or London. The pricing was in dollars, the features were designed for 50-person sales teams, and the onboarding assumed you had a dedicated IT department.

Meanwhile, a hardware shop owner in Nairobi, a logistics company in Mombasa, or a growing agency in Kisumu — they all had the same problem: leads falling through the cracks, no clear picture of the pipeline, follow-ups forgotten.

Azayon is the answer to that. Simple enough to set up in an afternoon. Powerful enough to run a serious sales operation. Priced so it makes sense for your business.

What we believe

Good software should get out of your way. The best CRM is the one your team actually uses — not the one with the most features, but the one that fits how you work.

African businesses deserve first-class tools built for their context — local pricing, local payment methods, and an understanding of how business gets done here.
